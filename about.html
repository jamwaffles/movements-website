About Waples Machine


Founded in 2020 by James Waples, Waples Machine is on a mission to create a best in class, open source, safe machine controller and ecosystem to empower users to control industrial machining centers at low cost.

Many such motion controllers already exist, but none that:

  • Are fully modular and encourage modification and expansion
  • Are written end-to-end in the Rust programming language
  • Provide a robust EtherCAT master written in Rust

That's where Movements comes in. Leveraging the safety, ease of use and performance of Rust, we believe we can fill a gap in the market with an excellent machine controller solution.
