Introducing Movements

Rapid solutions

Movements is our solution to a wide range of industrial motion control needs. Built from the ground-up to be modular and easily extended, it provides a solid base to create custom motion control solutions, whether that be new product offerings or older machine retrofits.

Support for a wide variety of hardware

Movements supports many fieldbus systems such as Modbus TCP and CANOpen, and adheres closely to existing industrial safety protocols. This allows Movements to use and grow a rich ecosystem of connected devices, perfect for modern industry.

Rock solid safety

Movements is written in the Rust programming language, providing a safe, fast and secure solution for peace of mind and excellent performance.

Tailored expertise

Design and machine retrofits are our speciality. Let us do the hard work for you.

With many fieldbusses supported including Modbus TCP, CANopen and Profinet, our control retrofit package is a flexible and affordable upgrade path with minimal electrical changes required.

Development partner

Use our turnkey solution to interface with your existing systems, with support to back you up when you need it.

A wide variety of PC systems from full desktops to resource constrained embedded systems are supported, allowing Movements to be adapted to any customer need.
