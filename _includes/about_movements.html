About Movements

Movements is an open-source machine controller, closely integrating with existing industrial devices through the EtherCAT protocol, as well as other widely used fieldbus systems.

Movements is currently being developed internally at Waples Machine to enable us to provide industry with versatile motion control solutions and support at a low cost. When it's ready, Movements will also be released to the general public for use in personal and hobby projects.
