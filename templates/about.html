About Waples Machine


Founded in 2020 by James Waples, Waples Machine is on a mission to create a best in class, safe motion controller and ecosystem to empower users to control industrial machines at low cost.

Movements, our motion control solution, leverages the safety, ease of use and performance of Rust to fill a niche in the market; a software platform with reliability at its core.
